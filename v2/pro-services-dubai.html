What Corporate PRO Services Cover in Dubai

A corporate public relations officer, commonly called a PRO, coordinates recurring company work with UAE government departments and approved service channels. The role helps owners and managers keep licences, labour files, immigration records and supporting documents moving together.

A good PRO service gives the company one accountable desk for many connected government processes.

Labour and employment support

PRO work can include MOHRE and Tasheel applications, labour contracts, work permits, quota-related steps, WPS assistance and employee file updates.

Immigration and residency support

GDRFA, ICP and Amer services can include establishment and immigration records, entry permits, status changes, visa processing, Emirates ID coordination, renewals and cancellations.

Company and premises administration

Recurring support may also cover trade licence renewals and amendments, partner changes, Ejari, municipality coordination, RTA services, insurance and selected RERA-related documentation.

Why outsource PRO work?

  • One contact tracks deadlines and document requirements.
  • Connected applications are prepared in the right sequence.
  • Owners and internal teams spend less time visiting service centres.
  • Company, labour and immigration records are easier to keep aligned.
