About Us

Four decades of trusted document clearing.

Since 1983, Alwahaa Documents Clearing has been the bridge between clients and UAE government departments — guiding entrepreneurs, families and established companies through licensing, visas, attestation and PRO procedures with calm precision.

Our story

One trusted desk for every UAE approval.

From the first trade licence application to renewals, amendments, attestations, labour support, tax assistance and bank account coordination, Alwahaa works as a single counter for UAE paperwork, approvals and corporate support.

Our strength is simple: deep local expertise, fast processing and a trusted network earned over decades — so clients are never left navigating departments alone.

Founder's Message

A message from our founder.

“

For more than four decades, I have had the privilege of calling the United Arab Emirates my home and witnessing the extraordinary transformation of Dubai into one of the world's most dynamic and prosperous cities.

When I established Alwahaa Group more than 40 years ago, it was built upon a simple vision — to provide trustworthy, reliable, and professional business support services to entrepreneurs, investors, families, and businesses seeking to make the UAE their home for growth and success.

Throughout these years, I have been fortunate to witness the remarkable journey of this nation and contribute, in our own humble way, to the aspirations of thousands of clients from around the world. As Dubai evolved into a global hub of innovation and opportunity, Alwahaa Documents Clearing grew alongside it, guided by the same values that shaped us from the beginning: integrity, commitment, and personalized service.

Today, while embracing modern solutions and new opportunities, we remain deeply committed to the principles upon which this company was founded. Every client who walks through our doors is treated with respect, sincerity, and dedication, because we believe that lasting relationships are built on trust.

On behalf of the entire Alwahaa family, I thank you for your confidence in us and look forward to being part of your journey in the United Arab Emirates.

Sarubutheen Abdul RahmanFounder & CEO, Alwahaa Group
