UAE Family Visa and Emirates ID Process

Eligible UAE residents can sponsor qualifying family members. The exact documents and steps depend on the sponsor's status, the relationship, the applicant's location and the rules of the relevant immigration authority.

Family residency is a connected process: sponsorship, medical fitness where required, Emirates ID and final residency formalities must align.

Common stages in the process

  1. Confirm sponsor and dependent eligibility.
  2. Prepare relationship, identity, income and accommodation evidence.
  3. Apply for the appropriate entry or status process.
  4. Complete medical fitness testing when required.
  5. Submit Emirates ID and residency applications.

Documents often requested

  • Passports and photographs for sponsor and dependants
  • Attested marriage or birth certificates
  • Sponsor visa, Emirates ID and employment or company records
  • Tenancy or accommodation evidence and any authority-specific forms

Renewals and cancellations

Residency and Emirates ID records should be renewed before expiry. Changes in employment, sponsorship or family status can also require cancellation, transfer or a new application.
