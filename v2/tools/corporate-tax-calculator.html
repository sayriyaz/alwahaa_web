  • 0% on taxable income up to AED 375,000; 9% on the portion above, for financial years starting on or after 1 June 2023.
  • Small Business Relief: revenue ≤ AED 3M may elect to be treated as having no taxable income (through the period ending 31 Dec 2026).
  • Free zone: a Qualifying Free Zone Person can get 0% on qualifying income, 9% on non-qualifying income, if conditions are met.
  • Large multinationals (group revenue ≥ EUR 750M) may fall under the 15% Domestic Minimum Top-up Tax — outside this estimate.

Indicative estimate only — not tax or legal advice. UAE corporate tax treatment depends on your specific structure, elections, exempt income and Federal Tax Authority guidance. Figures current as of June 2026. For an accurate assessment and registration/filing support, contact the Alwahaa desk.
