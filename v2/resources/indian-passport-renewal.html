Passport Renewal for Indian citizens in the UAE

Indian Embassy and Consulate services in the UAE support Indian citizens and applicants with passport renewal or reissue, Police Clearance Certificate (PCC), visa, attestation and other consular services.

Important update: until the end of June 2026, outsourced Indian passport services in the UAE are handled by the existing provider network, including BLS International. From 1 July 2026, the Government of India is transitioning outsourced passport, visa and attestation services across the UAE to Al Hind Tours & Travel LLC.

Because this is a transition period, the current official provider may depend on the appointment date and service type. Always confirm the latest booking channel with the Embassy of India, Consulate General of India or the official outsourced service provider before visiting.

Services

  • Indian passport renewal and reissue
  • Police Clearance Certificate (PCC)
  • Indian visa services
  • Document attestation and consular services
  • Appointment booking guidance through the official provider
  • Emergency consular assistance guidance

When do you need Indian passport renewal support?

  • Your Indian passport is expiring, expired, damaged or has no blank pages.
  • You need a passport reissue for name, address, spouse or personal-detail changes.
  • You need a PCC for immigration, employment or residency requirements.
  • You require embassy or consular attestation for Indian documents.
  • You are booking during the June-July 2026 provider transition and need the correct official channel.

Frequently asked questions

Where do Indian citizens renew passports in the UAE?

Indian passport renewal in the UAE is handled through the official outsourced service provider appointed by the Government of India. Until the end of June 2026, services are handled by the existing provider network, including BLS International. From 1 July 2026, Al Hind Tours & Travel LLC is scheduled to take over outsourced passport, visa and attestation services across the UAE. Check the official provider before booking.

Is there a pause during the provider transition?

Reports based on the Embassy announcement say passport, visa and attestation services are paused from 26 June to 30 June 2026 for the transition, while emergency services continue. For urgent cases, contact the Embassy or Consulate through official channels.

Can Alwahaa complete the process for me?

Alwahaa can help you prepare documents, understand the checklist, coordinate booking guidance and support related attestation or administrative paperwork. The official application, appointment and final decision remain with the Indian Embassy, Consulate and appointed service provider.
