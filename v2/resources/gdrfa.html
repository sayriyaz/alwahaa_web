GDRFA — General Directorate of Residency & Foreigners Affairs — Dubai

Dubai residence visas, visit visas, Golden Visa and entry permits.

What is GDRFA?

GDRFA Dubai — the General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs — manages residency permits, visas, Golden Visa services, entry permits and immigration matters specifically within the Emirate of Dubai.

While ICP handles federal identity and residency in several emirates, residence-visa processing for Dubai is done through GDRFA Dubai. Most Dubai residents deal with GDRFA when a visa is issued, renewed, sponsored or cancelled.

Services

  • Residence visas (new, renewal, cancellation)
  • Visit and tourist visas
  • Golden Visa services
  • Entry permits and visa status
  • Family sponsorship
  • Smart Gate registration

When do you need GDRFA?

  • You are applying for or renewing a Dubai residence visa.
  • You are sponsoring family members on a Dubai visa.
  • You need to cancel a residence visa in Dubai.
  • You are applying for a visit or tourist visa.
  • You want to register for Smart Gate for faster airport passage.

Frequently asked questions

How do I check my visa status in Dubai?

You can check Dubai residence and visit visa status on the GDRFA Dubai website or app using your file number or passport details. Typing centres and Amer service centres can also check for you.

ICP or GDRFA — which one do I use for a Dubai visa?

For residence visas processed in Dubai, GDRFA Dubai is the authority. ICP issues the Emirates ID and handles federal services and residency in several other emirates. Many Dubai applications touch both.

What is Smart Gate?

Smart Gate is an automated passport-control system at Dubai airports that lets registered residents and eligible travellers pass immigration quickly using their Emirates ID or passport.
