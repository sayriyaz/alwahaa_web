🇦🇪 Identity & Immigration

ICP — Federal Authority for Identity, Citizenship, Customs & Port Security

Emirates ID, federal residency, Golden Visa, entry permits and citizenship.

What is ICP?

ICP — the Federal Authority for Identity, Citizenship, Customs and Port Security — is the UAE federal body that manages national identity and immigration records. It issues the Emirates ID, runs the population register, and handles residency, entry permits, Golden Visa and citizenship matters at federal level.

In Dubai, day-to-day residency visas are handled by GDRFA, while ICP manages Emirates ID nationwide and residency in several other emirates. Most residents deal with ICP whenever an Emirates ID is issued, renewed or replaced.

Services

  • Emirates ID issuance, renewal and replacement
  • Residence visa processing (federal emirates)
  • Golden Visa applications
  • Entry permits and visa status changes
  • UAE citizenship and federal passport services
  • Population register and identity records

When do you need ICP?

  • You are issued a new residence visa and need an Emirates ID.
  • Your Emirates ID is expiring and must be renewed (usually within 30 days of expiry).
  • You are applying for a Golden Visa or long-term residency.
  • You need an entry permit or to change your visa status from inside the country.
  • You have lost or damaged your Emirates ID and need a replacement.

Frequently asked questions

How long does an Emirates ID take?

After your biometrics (fingerprints) are captured, the Emirates ID is typically printed within a few working days to about two weeks, depending on the application type and any approvals required. Urgent (fast-track) services can be faster.

How do I check my Emirates ID or application status?

You can track the status on the ICP website or the ICP UAE smart app using your application (PRAN) number or Emirates ID number. Typing centres can also check on your behalf.

How do I renew my Emirates ID?

Renew within 30 days of expiry through the ICP app or website, or at an approved typing centre. You will usually need a valid residence visa, passport copy and the renewal fee; new biometrics may be requested.
