🇦🇪 Employment & Labour

MOHRE — Ministry of Human Resources & Emiratisation

Work permits, labour contracts, WPS and workplace dispute support.

What is MOHRE?

MOHRE — the Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation — is the UAE federal authority that regulates the private-sector labour market. It governs work permits, labour contracts, the Wages Protection System (WPS), workplace disputes and Emiratisation targets.

Almost every private-sector company in the UAE deals with MOHRE when hiring staff, issuing or renewing work permits, registering labour contracts and ensuring salaries are paid through WPS.

Services

  • Work permits (new, renewal, cancellation)
  • Labour contracts and offer letters
  • Wages Protection System (WPS) registration
  • Labour complaints and dispute resolution
  • Salary and wage protection compliance
  • Emiratisation and quota services

When do you need MOHRE?

  • You are hiring an employee and need a work permit and labour contract.
  • An employee's work permit or labour card is up for renewal.
  • Your company must register salaries through the Wages Protection System (WPS).
  • You need to file or respond to a labour complaint or dispute.
  • You are checking Emiratisation requirements or quotas for your company.

Frequently asked questions

What is WPS?

The Wages Protection System is an electronic salary-transfer system that lets MOHRE confirm employees are paid correctly and on time through approved banks and exchange houses. Most private-sector companies must pay salaries via WPS.

How do I file a labour complaint?

You can register a labour complaint with MOHRE through their app, website, call centre or an approved service centre. MOHRE reviews the case and may refer unresolved disputes to the labour court.

How do I check a work permit or labour contract status?

Work permit and labour contract status can be checked on the MOHRE app or website using the transaction or person details, or through a Tasheel service centre.
