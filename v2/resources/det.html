🇦🇪 Business & Licensing

DET — Dubai Department of Economy & Tourism

Dubai mainland trade licences, trade names and commercial registration.

What is DET?

DET — the Dubai Department of Economy and Tourism (formerly the DED) — issues mainland trade licences, approves business activities, reserves trade names and manages commercial registration in Dubai.

Any mainland company in Dubai deals with DET to start, renew or amend its trade licence and to add or change business activities.

Services

  • Trade licence issuance
  • Trade name reservation
  • Initial approval
  • Business activity search
  • Licence renewal and amendment

When do you need DET?

  • You are starting a mainland company in Dubai.
  • You need to reserve a trade name or get initial approval.
  • Your trade licence is due for renewal.
  • You want to add activities or amend your licence.
  • You are checking which activities your business needs.

Frequently asked questions

How do I get a Dubai mainland trade licence?

Mainland licensing through DET typically involves choosing your activity, reserving a trade name, obtaining initial approval, completing documentation and Ejari, then collecting the licence. Steps vary by activity and legal form.

What is the difference between DED and DET?

DET is the current name for what many still call the DED (Department of Economic Development). It is the same Dubai authority for economic licensing, now under the Department of Economy and Tourism.

How do I renew a DET trade licence?

A mainland licence is renewed through DET, usually requiring a valid Ejari tenancy and settlement of any fees. Alwahaa can prepare and submit the renewal on your behalf.
